Profile

I am interested in programming and statistics, interests that stem from a career in academia where I used R on a near daily basis for data processing, visualisation and analysis. In 2020, I decided to put those interests at the center of my career, joining the AstraZeneca Late R&I Biometrics Graduate Program as a Programmer in 2020. I continue to develop my skills as a programmer through work-based training and private study; I am currently learning Java, while Python, JavaScript and C++ are all high on my list of priorities.

Outside of work, I follow and participate in a number of sports including cycling, football, running, cross-country skiing and squash. Most of my free time is spent with my partner and our three small children (Nils, Olof and Sven) enjoying the Swedish countryside.
